Top 10 Countries on Netflix

This bar chart highlights the top 10 countries with the largest number of Netflix titles. Each bar represents the total number of titles (movies and TV shows combined) available in a specific country. Hover over a bar to see the title count.

Top 10 Countries: Movie & TV Show Split

This stacked bar chart shows the split between movies and TV shows for the top 10 countries on Netflix. Each bar represents the proportion of movies (in orange) and TV shows (in purple) available in that country. Percentages are displayed within each segment.

Netflix Movies & TV Shows Over Time

This area chart visualizes the number of Netflix movies and TV shows released each year from 2008 to 2020. The orange area represents movies, while the purple area represents TV shows. Hover over the chart to explore trends for specific years.
